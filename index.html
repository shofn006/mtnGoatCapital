OVERVIEW

mtn. GOAT CAPITAL mgmt. (Goat Capital) is a Sheridan, Wyoming based holding company that invests in startup and micro-middle market companies domiciled in the United States and abroad. Using proprietary search we seek companies with identifiable growth opportunities across a wide array of industries. Specifically, we look for individual or family-owned-and-operated businesses that have a need for liquidity or desire a strategic partner to help grow and scale the business for next-level success.

With an office in Tokyo, Japan we are active in the Asian markets as well as those at home.

APPROACH

MICRO-MIDDLE MARKET COMMITMENT

Small businesses are principally overlooked by private equity firms as they seek lower-middle market and larger deals. This results in a discrepancy between the number of businesses looking for partnerships and the amount of capital and value-add available to them.

TARGET ALIGNMENT

By virtue of Goat Capital's operating and investment experience we provide business owners with needed liquidity, flexibility, or succession planning based on their desired outcomes. With boots-on-the-ground team involvement we are fully committed to sustained prosperity of customers, employees and shareholders of the business.

GROWTH DRIVEN

Working cheek by jowl with management post a successful transaction, strategic and tactical execution is our value creation mechanism. Establishing inefficient processes into efficient ones, procuring lower supply chain costs, improving existing technology and leveraging network and industry expertise are amongst a host of activities that are crucial to the success of the business.
