OVERVIEW

mtn. GOAT CAPITAL mgmt. (Goat Capital) is a Las Vegas, Nevada based holding company that invests in startup and micro-middle market companies domiciled in the United States and abroad. Using proprietary search we seek companies with identifiable growth opportunities across a wide array of industries. Specifically, we look for individual or family-owned-and-operated businesses that have a need for liquidity or desire a strategic partner to help grow and scale the business for next-level success.

With an office in Tokyo, Japan we are active in the Asian markets as well as those at home.
